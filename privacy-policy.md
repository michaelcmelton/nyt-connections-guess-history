# Privacy Policy for Connections Guess History Extension

## Overview
The Connections Guess History extension is designed to enhance your experience with the New York Times Connections game by tracking and displaying your guess history. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection and Usage
Our extension:
- Only operates on the New York Times Connections game page
- Only reads game-related data from your browser's local storage
- Does not collect or transmit any personal information
- Does not use cookies or tracking mechanisms
- Does not store any data on external servers

### Specific Data Handled
The extension only accesses:
- Your current game state from local storage
- The current game's word choices displayed on the page
- Your guess history for the current game

All data processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

## Data Storage
- All game data is stored locally in your browser
- Data is automatically cleared when you start a new game
- You can remove all stored data by uninstalling the extension

## Third-Party Access
We do not:
- Share any data with third parties
- Collect analytics or usage statistics
- Use any external services or APIs

## Changes to This Policy
Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in an updated version distributed with the extension.

## Contact
For any questions about this privacy policy or the extension's data handling practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support channel.
